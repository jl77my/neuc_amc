Organiser Australian Mathematics Trust
Objectives
  1. To highlight the importance of Mathematics as a school curriculum;
  2. To give students an opportunity to discover their talent in Mathematics;
  3. To provide resources for students to engage in discussions in the classroom or in the exchange.
