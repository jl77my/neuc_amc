Other's Feedbacks


Here are some of the feedbacks we have received from participants of the NEUC Australian Mathematics Competition:

Annie

Student (Junior Year 7)

I loved participating in the Australian Mathematics Competition! The questions were tricky but fun and it made me think in new ways. I was so proud when I solved some of the harder ones. Can't wait for next year!

Lim Xuan Yi

Student (Senior Year 11)

Participating in the NEUC Australian Mathematics Competition as a Year 11 student was an incredibly rewarding experience. The questions were challenging and required a deep understanding of mathematical concepts. The competition helped me enhance my problem-solving skills and prepare for future academic challenges. The feedback and review sessions were particularly beneficial in identifying my strengths and areas for improvement. I highly recommend it to anyone looking to push their mathematical abilities.

Terry

Student (Middle Primary Year 4)

I had so much fun in the Australian Mathematics Competition! I learned a lot of new things from it. Thank you New Era University College for organizing AMC! I can't wait to join again next year!

Cheng Chih Kan

Student (Intermediate Year 9)

The Australian Mathematics Competition was like a thrilling math adventure! Each question felt like unlocking a new level of a game, and the challenge made it all the more exciting. I really enjoyed diving deep into complex problems and discovering new strategies.

Michelle Lew

Student (Upper Primary Year 6)

I had an amazing time at the Australian Mathematics Competition! The math challenges were super fun and made me feel like a math superhero. I loved figuring out the tricky problems and seeing how much I could learn.
