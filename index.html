About Us

New Era University College

Australian Mathematics Competition

At New Era University College, we take a unique approach. Instead of solely focusing on solving problems, we concentrate on creating them. Why do we do this? Because we believe that the future hinges on our ability to confront and overcome challenges. Every day, we develop new challenges for students, helping them sharpen their problem-solving abilities to be ready for real-world issues. One of our key initiatives is organizing the Australian Mathematics Competition, providing students with the opportunity to practice and excel in solving complex problems.
