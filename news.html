News

  1. The Australian Mathematics Competition will be held on 08 August 2024 (Thursday) at 11:00 am at various participating schools. Anyone interested can sign up with their school authorities.
  2. The participating schools are responsible for providing a competition venue in the school premises and appointing teachers from the school or agency as invigilators for the competition.
  3. The entry fee for each participant is RM 43.
  4. The competition is participated by Online Registration. Please proceed to the Online Registration Website (https://fict.newera.edu.my/) to sign up for an account.
  5. Please log in to the Online Registration Website and fill in the form correctly.
  6. Please make the correct payment according to the NEUC bank account detail provided in the Online Registration Form. Upload the proof of payment via the Online Registration Form and make sure all the fill-in details are complete and correct before clicking submit.
  7. The Closing date of online registration will be 12 July 2024 (Friday).
  8. The Australian Mathematics Competition Working Committee of New Era University College (NEUC) will deliver the AMC competition papers and answer sheets directly to the competition venue. Immediately after the competition, the school are required to put the answer sheets in a plastic envelope and arrange the courier service to send the parcel to New Era University College.
  9. Feel free to contact the Australian Mathematics Competition Working Committee, NEUC - 03-8740 6392 / 03-8210 3709 ext.: 418 if you have any inquiries.
